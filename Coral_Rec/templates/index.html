About

There are over 6,000 species of coral around the world. Many of them are already a part of the reef-keeping hobby, while others remain a mystery. From hobbyists that have kept coral for decades to those planning on getting their first tank, we can all learn about new corals. This website serves as a tool to help plan, visualize, and research new corals that may be great additions to your aquarium. With a growing, curated list of many coral species as well as details about their care requirements, this site hopes to inspire hobbyists to try new things.
